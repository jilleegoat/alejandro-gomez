Alejandro Gomez

In the fashion of an introvert, I will try to keep it short and purposeful because there's nothing worse than idle chatter, especially since I am new to promoting myself.

Welcome to my artistic journey. Much like any introductions, you need a reason why you are looking at this right now. I can go as grand as to say I am just some spirit looking for a human experience, or I can simply say I am an artist looking for work.

Illustrating and inking my own work is nothing short of fulfilling and making weird music is second nature of the divine kind. Getting to know other like-minded souls and diving deep into words and memories worthy to be recorded. I am a weird dude. Being weird entails not taking myself seriously.

To me, this will always be amateur hour. No way can I admit that I know what I am doing, yet I keep pushing myself to do it because I keep asking "Why be a storyteller? What makes you think tomorrow exists? Why not now?' Nothing is permanent. Especially this page. However, if you've made it this far, thank you! It means so much to me. Please take a look through the pages above. I'm going to try and get into 'blogging' so prepare yourself. You've been warned. Thanks, everyone! Talk soon!
